Reasoning:

We came up with this app because as current student we noticed many students roaming the halls during class time this would cause many clusters of students crowding the available restrooms. Because of this we questioned how will students be able to use the restroom when full of others just trying to skip class.

Add-ons:

Uses for this app

Use 1

Teachers will be able to direct their students to the nearest available restrooms leading to less wondering students in the halls.

Use 2

Students will have an easier time finding which restrooms are available as some days different restrooms are oped or closed.

Use 3

This can help AP/Administration find students that want to skip in the restrooms or students that are participating in suspicious activities
